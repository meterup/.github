# Meter

We make it easy for organizations to get great internet, networking, and Wi-Fi.

### What we’re doing

Meter builds enterprise networks from the ground up. We design our own hardware, write our own firmware, build our own software and models, manage our own deployments, and run our own support.

It's a single, integrated solution that scales from branch offices, warehouses, and large campuses to data centers.

### Why it matters

The internet runs the world. Every email you send, purchase you make, video call you join: it's all packets flowing through networks. But those networks haven't changed for decades. They're fragmented, manual, and difficult to operate.

Our bet with Meter is that we’ll all use the internet more than we do today. We’re building the definitive networking stack to enable businesses to do so as seamlessly and reliably as any modern utility.
